Research Interest

Core Focus

My research interests are centered on the integration of Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Computer Vision, with a strong emphasis on developing robust and interpretable models for visual understanding.

I am particularly interested in image enhancement and reconstruction techniques that improve feature quality and representation learning in degraded or resource-constrained environments.

My current focus includes applying these methods to automated disease detection in both medical imaging and agricultural vision systems, where noise, blur, and low-resolution inputs significantly affect model performance. Through this work, I aim to design intelligent systems that are accurate, reliable, and deployable in real-world low-resource settings.
